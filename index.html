Experiences

Business Intelligence Intern - Sacramento Kings

This summer, I had the incredible opportunity to intern with the Sacramento Kings as part of their Business Intelligence team. During my time there, I honed my skills in SQL, R, and Tableau while gaining valuable insights into the inner workings of an NBA team. I learned how data drives critical business decisions and how the organization operates behind the scenes. One of my key projects involved creating efficient, long-lasting dashboards in Tableau for the marketing department to evaluate the success of events at the Golden 1 Center. This experience allowed me to understand the importance of creating tools that not only provide immediate insights but also remain functional and relevant over time.

In addition to dashboard creation, I also developed a logistic regression model in R to predict the likelihood of season ticket members retaining their memberships for the next season. I integrated this model into a Shiny app, providing an interactive tool for the team to use in their decision-making process. SQL played a crucial role in both projects, enabling me to manage and manipulate the data effectively. I want to thank everyone who helped me get this opportunity and the entire team at the Kings for making this experience so rewarding. I'm excited to apply the skills and knowledge I've gained to future opportunities in sports analytics and business intelligence.
